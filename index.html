Privacy Policy for AltMAP

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal user information (such as name, email, or contact details)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Data from scanned devices

2. Local and Remote Scanning

Our app performs:

  • Local network scans using standard protocols
  • Remote network scans via user-specified IPs or domains
All scanning operations are initiated manually by the user and processed locally on the device.

3. Permissions Used

The app may request:

  • Internet access (for remote scanning)
  • Foreground service (to run long tasks visibly)
These permissions are only used for their stated purposes and not to collect data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.
